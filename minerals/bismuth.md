Lab-grown bismuth crystals are popular among collectors for their geometric beauty and vivid rainbow tarnish. Natural bismuth crystals are rare and typically less colorful. Bismuth is one of the least toxic heavy metals and is used in various pharmaceutical and cosmetic products.
