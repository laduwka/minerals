# Groups and Subclasses

Within each mineral class, minerals are further divided into groups based on structure and composition.

## Silicates

Silicates are subdivided by how SiOâ‚„ tetrahedra link together:

| Subclass | Structure | Examples |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| **Nesosilicates** | Isolated tetrahedra | Olivine, garnet |
| **Sorosilicates** | Double tetrahedra | Epidote, zoisite |
| **Cyclosilicates** | Rings | Beryl, tourmaline |
| **Inosilicates** | Single/double chains | Pyroxene, amphibole |
| **Phyllosilicates** | Sheets | Mica, clay minerals |
| **Tectosilicates** | 3-D framework | **Quartz**, feldspar |

## Sulfides

| Group | Examples |
|-------|----------|
| **Simple sulfides** | **Pyrite**, galena, sphalerite |
| **Sulfosalts** | Tetrahedrite, enargite |

## Carbonates

| Group | Examples |
|-------|----------|

| **Aragonite group** | Aragonite, cerussite |
